About Us

Where tails wag and pets play!

Welcome to AMT Pet shop! We are a team of passionate pet lovers dedicated to providing the best possible care and products for your furry friends. Our goal is to make your pet's life as happy and healthy as possible by offering a wide range of high-quality pet food, toys, accessories, and grooming services.

Thank you for choosing AMT Pet shop. We look forward to serving you and your furry friends!

Our Services

Vaccination

Our animal vaccination service provides essential protection for your furry friends. Our licensed veterinarians administer vaccines to prevent a range of diseases such as rabies, distemper, and parvo.

Pet grooming

Our shops have professional pet groomers on staff who can provide grooming services such as bathing, clipping, and trimming. Grooming services may be available for dogs, cats, and other small animals.

Pet photography

Our pet shops may offer professional pet photography services to help pet owners capture special moments with their furry friends.

Pet training

Our pet shops may offer training classes or individual training sessions to help pets learn obedience, socialization, and other skills.
